About the College

Dr. Raj Shekhar Shrivastava Para Medical College, located in Hiropatti, Jagban, Near Kamtaul, Madhubani (Bihar), India, is dedicated to providing high-quality education in the field of para-medical sciences.

Mission

To impact comprehensive education and training in paramedical sciences, fostering a new generation of healthcare professionals.

Vision

To be a leading paramedical institution recognized for excellence in education, training, and research.
